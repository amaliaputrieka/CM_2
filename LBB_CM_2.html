In this case, we would like to get true positive as much as we can and expect false negative with minimum numbers. This model also gives value of 96% in sensitivity as the matrix evaluation that will be fit to our case.

Summary

Having observed both of the models, Decision Tree has higher accuracy (90%) and sensitivity (96%). ‘model_dt’ is a better model that could overcome our business needs for classifying the list of loan customers. Hopefully, telemarketing can utilize this model for reaching the right loan customers and improve the sales value.
